# Appendix A: Search Strategy

## A.1 Databases Searched

The literature review was conducted using reputable academic databases and digital libraries, including:

* IEEE Xplore
* ACM Digital Library
* ScienceDirect (Elsevier)
* SpringerLink
* Google Scholar
* Scopus

## A.2 Search Keywords

The following search strings were used individually and in combination:

* "Machine Learning" AND "Cybersecurity"
* "Intrusion Detection System" AND "Machine Learning"
* "Network Intrusion Detection"
* "Cyber Threat Detection"
* "Random Forest" AND "Cybersecurity"
* "Support Vector Machine" AND "Intrusion Detection"
* "Deep Learning" AND "Network Security"

## A.3 Inclusion Criteria

* Peer-reviewed journal articles and conference papers
* Publications from 2021–2025 (with selected classical studies where necessary)
* Studies focusing on machine learning applications in cybersecurity
* English-language publications

## A.4 Exclusion Criteria

* Non-peer-reviewed articles
* Duplicate publications
* Studies unrelated to cybersecurity or machine learning
* Papers lacking sufficient methodological detail
